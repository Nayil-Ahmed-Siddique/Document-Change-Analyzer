Company Employee Work Policy – Version 1

1. Working Hours
Employees are required to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
All employees must be present in the office during these hours.

2. Remote Work
Remote work is not permitted except in emergency situations.
Any remote work request must be approved by the manager in advance.

3. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 paid leave days per year.
Unused leave days cannot be carried forward to the next year.

4. Data Security
Employees must not share company data with external parties.
All company systems must be accessed only from office devices.

5. Policy Updates
This policy is subject to change at the discretion of management.
Employees will be notified via email if changes are made.
