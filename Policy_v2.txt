Company Employee Work Policy – Version 2

1. Working Hours
Employees are expected to work 8 hours per day, Monday to Friday.
Flexible working hours are allowed with manager approval.

2. Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week.
Remote work requests must be submitted through the internal portal.

3. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 24 paid leave days per year.
Up to 5 unused leave days can be carried forward to the next year.

4. Data Security
Employees must not share confidential company data with unauthorized parties.
Company systems may be accessed from approved personal devices with security software installed.

5. Policy Updates
Policy changes will be communicated through email and the company dashboard.
Employees are responsible for reviewing updated policies.
